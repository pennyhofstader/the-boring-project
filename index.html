I am BORED.

Boredom and depression intertwine, casting shadows on my day, In this weary state, I struggle to find my way. Minutes stretch into hours, a relentless, heavy load, Lost in the depths of despair, on this desolate road.

Each passing moment feels like an eternity, devoid of thrill, In the silence, echoes of melancholy linger still. Longing for a spark, a glimmer of hope to ignite, To break free from this cycle, to emerge into the light.

Yet, in the depths of loneliness, there exists a flicker of hope, a yearning for the embrace of friendship and the promise of brighter days ahead.
